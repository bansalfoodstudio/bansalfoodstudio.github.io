About Bansal Food Studio

We cook with care — serving authentic and comforting dishes across Indian, South Indian, Chinese and Continental cuisines.

Our Story

Bansal Food Studio began with a simple idea: make honest, flavorful food accessible to families and visitors in Nanauta. We started small and focused on consistency — the same taste every day, friendly service, and clean, welcoming space. Our philosophy remains: “Dil Aur Pet Dono Khush”.

We believe in fresh ingredients, well-tested recipes, and respectful hospitality. Over time, the studio has become a local favorite for weekday lunches, family dinners and quick takeaways.

About Nanauta

Nanauta is a warm, bustling town near Saharanpur with a growing appetite for good food. We chose this location because it sits at the crossroads of families, commuters and students — people who value quick, tasty meals and genuine hospitality. Our restaurant is easy to find at Chawla Complex on Gangoh Road and we are proud to be part of the local community.

Our Chefs

Our chefs come from diverse culinary backgrounds — regional Indian kitchens, south-Indian specialists for dosas & idlis, and cooks trained in continental & Chinese styles. They bring both classical technique and practical speed required for a busy restaurant.

Head Chef — Mr. Sharma 12 years experience in multi-cuisine kitchens; focuses on taste balance and consistency.
Sous Chef — Ms. Rathi Trained in south-Indian breakfast and dosa techniques; manages breakfast & tawa items.

Our Kitchen & Standards

Hygiene and safety are non-negotiable. We maintain daily cleaning schedules, separate storage for raw and cooked food, and use filtered water for all food preparation. Our team follows safe-cooking temperatures and uses quality oils & packaging for deliveries.

We also regularly train staff on hygiene practices and food handling so you can enjoy meals that are both delicious and safe.

What Makes Us Special

We combine family recipes with modern presentation and consistent portions. Whether you want a hearty thali, a quick dosa or a bowl of noodles, we aim to deliver a satisfying plate every time.
