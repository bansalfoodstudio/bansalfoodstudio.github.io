Why Bansal Food Studio

Bansal Food Studio began with a simple idea — to bring honest taste, fresh ingredients and homestyle comfort to Nanauta. Our goal has always been to create a place where families, friends and travellers can enjoy food that feels warm, familiar and satisfying.

Every dish is made with attention, hygiene and consistency. We take pride in serving meals that are not only delicious but also crafted with care.

Nanauta — Our Home

Nanauta is a lively town known for its friendly community and growing food culture. Located along the Gangoh Road, it connects travellers, students and families — all looking for a clean and reliable place to dine.

We wanted to build a restaurant that matches the warmth of this town: simple, welcoming and genuinely flavourful.
