Our Story — How Bansal Food Studio Began

Bansal Food Studio started from a simple dream: to create a welcoming place in Nanauta where families and travellers can enjoy consistently good food from multiple cuisines. Our philosophy—"Dil Aur Pet Dono Khush"—guides every decision we make: fresh ingredients, balanced flavors, and warm service.


Why Nanauta?

Nanauta's friendly community and growing food culture inspired us. Located at Chawla Complex, Gangoh Road, we wanted to bring a clean, family-friendly dining option that serves local favorites along with a selection of continental and global dishes.

  • Easy access from main road and local neighborhoods
  • Comfortable seating for families and groups
  • Clean, quick service with takeaway & delivery

Our Kitchen – Clean, Safe & Quality-Assured

We follow strict food-safety and hygiene procedures:

  • Daily fresh supplies, proper storage and inventory routines
  • Filtered water for food preparation
  • Dedicated cleaning schedules, staff training and PPE
  • Safe cooking oil and temperature controls for frying/baking
